POVs and Experience Prototypes

In this assignment we revisit the findings from the needfinding and formulate points of view (POVs) for our potential users. We craft several How Might We (HMW) statements to frame the problem area and intended design goal. Based on the best HMW statements that frame the problem space, we brainstorm several solutions. Using the best of these solutions, we create and test three experience prototypes to further our needfinding and develop a better idea as to where to focus our project.

The solution to "HMW provide constructed information?" is using a sensor to track motion and nofity the users if they do wrong posture. In order to solve "HMW help them record their results?", we would like to provide an convinent function which can record their training efficiently. The most important HMW is "HMW help them staty motivated?", our solution is let users set up goal and create a cummulative reward that make them feel fulfilling and satisfying.
